Abuja, Nigeria - March 10, 2025

fg launches intiative to improve digital literacy in schools.

The Federal Government has launched a new intiative aimed at improving digital literacy among students in Nigerian schools. The program, tagged "Digital Nigeria", is designed to provide students with access to digital tools and resources, as well as training for teachers to integrate technology into their classrooms.

Lagos, Nigeria - March 5, 2025

Lagos State Government Launches Free Coding Program for Primary and Secondary School Students

The Lagos State Government has introduced a free coding program for primary and secondary school students, aimed at equipping them with essential digital skills. This initiative is part of the government's efforts to prepare young Nigerians for the digital age.

Abuja, Nigeria - February 28, 2025

Nigerian Government Launches Inclusive Education Initiative for Children with Special Needs.

The Nigerian Government has launched an inclusive education initiative aimed at providing equal access to quality education for children with special needs. This program seeks to bridge the gap in education opportunities for children with disabilities.

Abuja, Nigeria - March 8 2025

fg launches 'girls' education project' to boost enrollment and retenation rates

The federal Government has launched the 'Girls' Education Project' (GEP), a comprehensive initiative aimed at improving access to quality education for girls across Nigeria. This project seeks to address the disparities in educational opportunities and outcomes for girls.
